# Introduction

The Indian financial sector is on the brink of an AI revolution. Generative AI and advanced machine learning models are no longer just experimental capabilities—they are fundamental drivers of next-generation product strategy.

## Key Challenges

1. **Compliance & Regulation**
   Navigating the RBI's stringent guidelines on data privacy.
2. **Legacy Infrastructure**
   Integration with systems built decades ago.
3. **Talent Scarcity**
   Finding engineers capable of maintaining production-grade LLM architectures.

## AdvaitAI's Approach

We advocate for an incrementally deployed, **aligned AI model** that focuses on human-in-the-loop workflows rather than black-box automation. 

> "Intelligence augmentation, not just artificial intelligence, is the key to enterprise adoption."

### Conclusion

Our findings indicate a 35% reduction in operational friction when AI is used to augment rather than replace human agents in banking.
